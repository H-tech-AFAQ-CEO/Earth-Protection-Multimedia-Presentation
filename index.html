SHOCKING WATER FACT

Only 2.5% of Earth's water is fresh — and most is frozen. A 5-minute shower uses 60L of water. Turning off the tap while brushing teeth saves 8L per minute!

ENERGY ALERT

A single LED bulb uses 80% less energy than an old bulb. Leaving devices on standby wastes €100+/year per household. Unplug. Switch off. Save the future!

DID YOU KNOW?

Over 785 million people have no access to clean water. Fixing a dripping tap saves up to 20,000L per year — that's a swimming pool!

SOLAR POWER RISING

The sun delivers more energy in 1 hour than humanity uses in a whole year. Solar energy is growing by 25% annually. Renewables are the future — and the future is NOW.

CLIMATE & NATURE IN DANGER
The planet is sending an SOS. Are you listening?
+1.2°C
Earth has already warmed by 1.2°C since pre-industrial times. Scientists warn we must stay below 1.5°C.
1 Million
Species are at risk of extinction due to human activity — that's more than ever before in human history.
3.6mm/yr
Sea levels rise by 3.6 mm every year. By 2100, entire coastal cities could be submerged.
15 Billion
Trees are cut down every single year. That's 15 billion trees — about 46% of the world's forests gone since humans appeared.
The last 10 years were the hottest ever recorded on Earth. Climate change is NOT the future — it's happening right now.
ACTIONS YOU CAN TAKE TODAY
You are 14–15 years old. You already have the power to change the world.
Shorter Showers Cut your shower to 4 minutes. Save 200L+ every week!
Lights Out! Switch off lights and unplug chargers when not in use.
Walk or Bike Skip the car for short trips. Zero emissions = pure win.
Eat More Plants Meat production creates 14.5% of global greenhouse gases. One veggie day a week helps!
Sort Your Waste Use the right bin every single time. Teach your family too.
Plant Something A tree, a flower, a herb on the windowsill. Every green life matters.
